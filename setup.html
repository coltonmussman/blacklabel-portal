Let's set up your Drops.

A Drop is a lead delivered to you the moment a consumer hits submit — exclusively yours, on the days you choose to work. This takes about two minutes and tells the system exactly where, what, and when to send you leads.

Which states do you want leads from?

These are the states your license covers. Pick the ones you want to actively receive Drops from. Licensed somewhere new? Add it on your profile once the license clears.

What do you want to write?

Pick the product lines you want leads for. Final Expense is the most common; choose any that fit how you sell.

Which days do you want Drops?

Leads only come on the days you pick. The Drop is about speed — choose the days you're actually working and ready to call back fast.

Now build your card.

The instant a lead is assigned to you, National Coverage Group sends them this card — your name, photo, and number — so the call you make is one they're expecting, not a cold call. A finished card is required before we start sending you leads.

A clean, professional headshot matters — it's the first thing your lead sees. No good one? AI headshots are cheap: HeadshotPro (~$29) or BetterPic (~$19, 4K).

Safe defaults: "Licensed Insurance Agent" or "Licensed Insurance Producer." Only use "Broker" if you're actually licensed/operating as one — some states regulate advertised titles.
