Common questions

Tap a question to open it.

Leads from the states and verticals you're licensed in and have switched on route to you automatically - no claiming, no racing. Each one shows up on your My Leads page and in your email within about two minutes of the customer hitting submit. Every lead is yours alone: one lead, one agent, forever.

We hold a 24-hour replace-or-credit standard for genuinely bad leads - disconnected or wrong number, fake contact info, or someone who clearly never asked for a quote. Open the lead in My Leads and use the flag option to request a credit; we review every flag personally. It's not for "they didn't answer yet" - it's for leads that were never reachable.

Head to the Tracker page. Under "Where do you want leads?" you can switch any licensed state or vertical on or off - handy if you only want to write in a few of the states you hold. Changes apply to new leads right away. (Turning everything off pauses your leads until you turn something back on.)

Email us and we'll update it. Because routing is tied to where you're actually licensed, we verify license changes against your record before turning a new state on - it keeps everyone compliant.

Silver is $400/week on the card you put on file. It bills automatically each week. If a payment fails or you cancel, your lead flow pauses right away and picks back up the moment your payment is current again - you never pay for a week of leads you didn't get.

Three quick things to check: (1) your states and verticals are switched on under the Tracker; (2) you haven't hit your weekly cap yet; and (3) your subscription is active. If all three look right and it's still quiet, your state may just be between leads - send us a note and we'll look.

Your weekly lead count resets every Monday. The profit tracker - invested, premium written, deals, and your goal ring - resets on the 1st of each month so every month starts fresh. Your monthly goal target carries over so you don't have to re-enter it.

On My Leads, set the lead's status to "Sold" and enter the annual premium. It flows straight into your Profit Tracker - return, cost per policy, and the goal ring all update automatically.
