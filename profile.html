States

You receive leads from the states switched on below - only states you're licensed in (NIPR). Switch a state off to stop its leads right away. Switch one on to add it; added coverage starts at the beginning of your next paid week.

Need to add or remove a licensed state itself? Email blacklabelleads@gmail.com - we verify license changes before updating.
