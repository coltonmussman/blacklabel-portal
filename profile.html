The instant a lead is assigned to you, National Coverage Group sends them this card — your name, photo, and number — so when you call, they already know who you are. It turns a cold call into an expected one. A finished card (photo + call-from number) is required before we send you leads.

A clean, professional headshot matters — it's the first thing your lead sees. No good one? AI headshots are cheap: HeadshotPro (~$29) or BetterPic (~$19, 4K).
